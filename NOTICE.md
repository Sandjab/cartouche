Cartouche is licensed under MIT (see `LICENSE`). The bundled assets

`vellum-davinci`, `botanical-floral`, and `blossom-kawai` theme

## Watermark PNGs

| File | Origin | Date generated |
|------|--------|----------------|
| `src/cartouche/watermarks/davinci.png` | Generated by **Nano Banana 2** (Google's Gemini 3 Pro Image model) | 2026-05 |
| `src/cartouche/watermarks/floral.png`  | Generated by **Nano Banana 2** (Google's Gemini 3 Pro Image model) | 2026-05 |
| `src/cartouche/watermarks/kawai.png`   | Generated by **Nano Banana 2** (Google's Gemini 3 Pro Image model) | 2026-05 |

The three images are the unmodified raw output of the model — no
training-data search-and-paste, no scanning of copyrighted source
material, no derivative work of an existing illustration. They are
embedded inline (base64 `data:` URI) into the rendered SVG, never
loaded from an external host.

For the purposes of redistribution, we consider these files to fall
under the same MIT license as the rest of Cartouche. If you have a
specific compliance question for your jurisdiction (the legal status of
AI-generated assets is an evolving topic, particularly around
US Copyright Office guidance and the EU AI Act's training-set
provenance rules), file an issue at
<https://github.com/Sandjab/cartouche/issues> and we'll add the answer
to this file.
